Coming to Columbia’s campus for the first time—or even the seventh—can be overwhelming. Getting lost as you head to class is common, but it can often feel like everyone else knows exactly what’s going on when every day seems different for you. While learning where all your classes are is something that will simply take time and practice, Spectrum has you covered with understanding Columbia’s sometimes convoluted calendar.
